Connected Abroad

An iOS app to help expats stay connected with family back home.

Features

  • Weekly photo sharing — Select a photo, get an AI-suggested message, and send directly to WhatsApp, Telegram, or iMessage
  • Smart notifications — Weekly reminders to send a message home
  • Message history — Track all messages sent
  • Important dates — Birthdays and custom dates with reminders
  • Weather back home — See the weather and local time in your home city
