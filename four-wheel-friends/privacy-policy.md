# Privacy Policy for Four Wheel Friends

**Last updated:** February 21, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Four Wheel Friends ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. Four Wheel Friends is developed by Maxime Van den Kerkhof.

## Our Privacy Commitment

**Four Wheel Friends is designed with privacy in mind.** We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to external servers. All your data stays on your device.

## Information We Access

The app may request access to the following device features:

### Photos
- **Purpose:** To select and store photos related to your vehicles
- **Storage:** Photos are stored locally on your device only
- **Sharing:** Photos are only shared when you explicitly choose to do so

### Notifications
- **Purpose:** To send reminders and updates
- **Storage:** Notification preferences are stored locally on your device

## Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using:
- UserDefaults for preferences and settings
- Local file storage for app data

## Third-Party Services

The app does not currently integrate with third-party services that collect your data.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data never leaves your device except when you explicitly choose to share content.

## Children's Privacy

Four Wheel Friends does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
